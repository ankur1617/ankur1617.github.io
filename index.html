I am a third-year Ph.D. student at Oklahoma State University, working on harmful algal blooms, cyanobacterial toxin production, and the role of sulfur stoichiometry in ecological and toxicological processes.

My research integrates molecular biology, ionomics, and environmental toxicology under an atom-first biology framework.
