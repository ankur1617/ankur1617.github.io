Teaching

Teaching Philosophy

My teaching philosophy emphasizes conceptual understanding, quantitative reasoning, and mechanistic thinking in biology. I aim to help students connect molecular- and organism-level processes to ecological and environmental outcomes.

Teaching Experience

Graduate Teaching Assistant for undergraduate biology and ecology laboratories at Oklahoma State University. Responsibilities include laboratory instruction, curriculum development, mentoring, and assessment.

Mentorship & Outreach

I actively mentor undergraduate researchers and participate in departmental and graduate student initiatives aimed at improving scientific training and inclusion.
