Research

Research Overview

My research focuses on understanding how elemental nutrient availability—particularly sulfur— regulates cyanobacterial physiology and toxin production in freshwater ecosystems.

Current Themes

Sulfur-mediated regulation of microcystin biosynthesis, harmful algal blooms, ecological stoichiometry, and environmental toxicology.
